Poem: Pineal Gland

November 28, 2022

What is the question that needs an answer? When you donâ€™t live but you just exist. When there is too much chaos there are too many lies. Tell me what you see so I can tell you who you are! What is the question that needs an answer? What electrifies your soul, elevates your spirit? What makes love grow in your marrow? What is the question that needs an answer? What can you achieve without a vision? And when a vision owns you, Can you trust your decisions? If you got no aim why ask for precision? What is the question that needs an answer? When you transmute your pain, your soul. When you are seated in the seat of self. What is the question that needs an answer? When there is too much chaos there are too many lies. So open your eyes! Envision the vision of love, of self, of God. Make with precision, decisions to liberate your smile.
